User Testing For Regular People

User testing is just what a regular person finds while using technology. Are they able to figure out what to do on a website or an application in order to get to the place that the company wants them to. If regular people can't use a website... If regular people can't use an application... then,the company basically failed. It's something that actual tech people don't do because they know tech too much. They are too close to it.
